    How the signals are drawn.
    Projected topper = Test 04 total at or above 480/720. Building momentum = a gain of 30+ marks over Test 03. Needs improvement = a fall of 45+ marks from a Test-03 base of 380+, or a Test-04 total under 285 without a strong upward move. A student can carry more than one signal — e.g. A Anbalagan is both a topper and the second-biggest climber. Averages compare the 54 students common to both papers; ranks are centre ranks.
